WASTE CRAFT


EVENT DESCRIPTION :

  • Team should contain maximum 3 members.
  • They can use any waste materials of their own interest.
  • The participants should bring the required materials by their own.
  • They have to make the craft on the event place allocated.
  • Participants will be judged on creativity, utilization of resources, development of eco-friendly products and design aspects.
  • All participants will be awarded with certificate of participation.
  • All the crafts registered will be kept in extended forum.
